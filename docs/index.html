The problem

Prepaid services are tracked with spreadsheets, POS notes, memory, and vibes. Customers can’t verify what they have. Businesses can’t prove what happened. Drama follows.

The shift

Treat prepaid value like credits: issued with terms, tracked as events, redeemed with rules. The ledger becomes the source of truth—calm, consistent, and boring in the best way.

The outcome

Cleaner operations, fewer disputes, and a system that scales across providers — from hair to lawn care to anything service-backed.

How it works

  1. Issue credits

    Customer purchases prepaid value. A CREDIT_ISSUED event is recorded with timestamp, actor, and reason.

  2. Track balances

    Balances are computed from the ledger. No silent edits. Every change has a trail.

  3. Redeem services

    Services are redeemed with CREDIT_REDEEMED events tied to fulfillment records.

  4. Resolve disputes

    Disputes are handled with explicit events and reason codes. The story stays intact.

Ledger-first by design

Clear Credits uses an append-only event model. That means your system is explainable: every balance has a reason for existing.

FAQ

Is this crypto?

No. Clear Credits is a system for service-backed prepaid value. It is not an investment product, not a bank, and not a promise of profit.

Why not just use gift cards?

Gift cards often lack service-specific rules, granular audit trails, and dispute-ready event history. Clear Credits is ledger-first and rules-driven.

What kinds of services?

Anything service-backed: hair, nails, lawn care, home services, wellness packages, retainers—where prepaid value must be tracked and redeemed cleanly.
